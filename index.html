Interactive Chess
I created this chess board after finishing a command-line version of it and deciding that I wanted to actually see the board. The logic was originally written in Ruby, but I used a tool called Opal to convert my code into Javascript. From there it was just a matter of adding some html/css. You can check out its repository over on my github. Feel free to play around with the board. Piece rules have been implemented, meaning that rooks can only go in perpindicular directions while bishops are restricted to diagonals. Kings have a "reach" of 1, while queens are unlimited. Additionally, you can not make moves that put your king in check and you can not leave your king in check. As of now, there is no computer AI to play against, but I'm considering it for a future project!
